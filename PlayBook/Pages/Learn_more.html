Imagine arriving in an unfamiliar place and wanting to join a game of football...

With PlayBook, you can easily find and join local games without the hassle of organizing participants. Register as a player, specify your position, preferred playing foot or hand, and other game details. PlayBook locates nearby fields and lets you sign up for games at specific times. Once enough players join, a game is automatically arranged. Players can rate each other and the facilities, creating a system that matches users by skill level. PlayBook makes it easy to enjoy spontaneous matches and connect with new people, wherever you are.
